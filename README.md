# Build-a-Planet: The History of Earth

An interactive visual journey through 4.6 billion years of Earth's history. Travel from the supernova remnants that seeded our solar system to the pale blue dot we call home today.

## [View Live Demo](https://amyleesterling.github.io/build-a-planet/)

## About

Build-a-Planet lets you scrub through twelve major eras of Earth's formation and evolution using an interactive timeline. Each era features a dynamic, animated planet visualization that transforms as you progress through time — from swirling nebula dust, through molten magma oceans and asteroid bombardments, to the emergence of oceans, life, continents, and the modern Earth.

### Eras

1. **Supernova Remnants** — 4.6 billion years ago
2. **Birth of Earth** — 4.54 billion years ago
3. **The Hadean** — 4.5 billion years ago
4. **Late Heavy Bombardment** — 4.1 billion years ago
5. **First Oceans** — 3.8 billion years ago
6. **First Life** — 3.5 billion years ago
7. **Great Oxidation Event** — 2.4 billion years ago
8. **Snowball Earth** — 700 million years ago
9. **Cambrian Explosion** — 541 million years ago
10. **Pangaea** — 335 million years ago
11. **The Big One** — 66 million years ago
12. **Pale Blue Dot** — Present day

## How to Use

Drag the timeline slider at the bottom of the screen or click anywhere on the timeline to travel between eras. Watch as the planet transforms in real time with animated transitions, particle effects, and atmospheric changes.
